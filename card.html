Bengal Tiger

Bengal tigers's roar can be heard up to 2 miles, making them one of the loudest creatures.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera Tigris Tigris
  • Average length: 3 meters
  • Average lifespan: 8 to 10 years
  • Habitat: India, Bangladesh and Sri Lanka

Bengal tigers are powerful nocturnal hunters. They can reach speeds up to 55 Km/Hr and they have night vision six times better than humans.
